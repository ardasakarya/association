About Our Association

Empowering professionals through collaboration, innovation, and excellence since 1985

Who We Are

We are a dynamic professional association dedicated to advancing excellence in our industry through innovative programs, networking opportunities, and continuous learning initiatives.

Our community brings together thought leaders, emerging professionals, and industry veterans to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and drive meaningful change across the sector.

5,000+ Active Members

Professionals across 50+ countries

Our Mission

To empower professionals through exceptional education, networking opportunities, and industry advocacy while fostering innovation and ethical practices that advance our collective success and societal impact.

Our Vision

To be the premier global association that shapes the future of our industry through transformative leadership, cutting-edge research, and sustainable practices that benefit professionals and communities worldwide.

Our Journey

Four decades of growth, innovation, and impact

1985

Foundation Established

Founded by 12 visionary professionals with a mission to create a unified voice for industry advancement.

1992

International Expansion

Opened our first international chapters in Europe and Asia, establishing global presence.
