Annual Charity Gala 2024

March 15, 2024
Grand Ballroom, Marriott Hotel

Event Description

Join us for an unforgettable evening at our Annual Charity Gala 2024, where community spirit meets elegant celebration. This year's gala brought together over 300 distinguished guests, community leaders, and philanthropists for a night dedicated to supporting local education initiatives.

The evening featured a silent auction with over 50 exclusive items, a three-course gourmet dinner prepared by renowned chef Marcus Williams, and inspiring speeches from scholarship recipients who have benefited from our community programs.

Thanks to the generous support of our attendees, we successfully raised $125,000 to fund educational scholarships for underprivileged students in our community. The event showcased the power of collective action and the impact we can make when we come together for a common cause.
